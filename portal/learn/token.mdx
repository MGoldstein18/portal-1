In this section, we describe two of those - tokens (as the **Token** component) and NFTs (as the **NFT Collection** component). Both **Token** and **NFT Collection** implement popular token standards and give you full control over the mechanics of your currencies and NFTs.

![](/assets/portal/docs/token.png)

## Token

**Token** extends the [ERC 20 token standard](https://eips.ethereum.org/EIPS/eip-20). The protocol lets you create n number of **Token** components. A token behaves like any other currency you may be familiar with e.g. [USDC](https://www.circle.com/en/usdc).

Under the hood, **Token** uses [OpenZeppelin's tested and audited ERC 20 token standard implementation](https://docs.openzeppelin.com/contracts/4.x/api/token/erc20#ERC20PresetMinterPauser). This gives you full control over the mechanics of your currency. You can choose to issue a given amount of currency to particular users, let users claim the currency upon meeting certain conditions, or anywhere in between.
